Digit Recorgnisation Model

Our digit recognition tool leverages machine learning algorithms like Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to accurately classify handwritten digits.

The platform is designed for ease of use, allowing users to upload or draw digits and get quick predictions through a Flask-based web interface.

This tool is intended for educational purposes and is not suitable for any official or critical recognition tasks.
